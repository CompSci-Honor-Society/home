Computer Science Honor Society

Who We Are

The Computer Science Honor Society (CSHS) at Valley View High School is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing the study and ethical practice of computer science. We celebrate academic excellence, foster collaboration, and serve our community through mentorship. We believe that technology has the power to uplift communities, and our goal is to help students develop both the skills and character to become future leaders in the field.

Our Mission

To empower students to grow as learners, leaders, and changemakers through the study and application of computer science.

What We Do

Our club activities blend technical learning, leadership development, and service: Peer Coding & Workshops: Hands-on sessions covering JavaScript . Community Mentorship: We mentor over 1,000 students at local middle schools, introducing them to coding and digital literacy.

Academic Excellence: Members are recognized for achievement in computer science and contribute to peer tutoring. Networking & Guest Speakers: Learn from professionals in the tech field. Creative Projects: Members work on websites, games, apps, and other real-world CS projects.
